Meet Emily

Introduction

I'm a designer hailing from the lovely city of Cleveland, OH. I moved to Austin in 2015 after I decided I never wanted to go back home after SXSW. After seven years here, I have no regrets! I love spending time with my family, singing karaoke, and trying out new design tools (check out my testimonial on UserBit's website).

Why Design?

I knew I wanted to be a designer from an early age. From fierce coloring contests in third grade to pursuing a design degree in college, it's always been my passion. Turning requirements into a tangible product creates a wonderful sense of accomplishment.

My Story

My path to becoming a designer did not take a traditional route. I have had the opportunity to take on various positions throughout my career. This has allowed me to take the skills I've learned in previous jobs and incorporate them in my design process.
