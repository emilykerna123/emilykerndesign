SiMMA

Introduction

SiMMA is a text based MMA simulation game were you will take on the role of a fighters managers. The long term goal is to occupy any of the roles in the MMA hierarchy.

Challenges

The main challenge is to create a text simulation app that incorporates an algorithm to determine what combinations win the fight.

Research

There are three personas we want to meet with our target market. The Fighter, Manager, and Promoter.
I did some research to see what other apps are in the market. There isn't a ton of competition for text based simulation games for MMA so there was a lot of opportunity. I collected research from the two main competitors, MMA Manager and MMA Tycoon.

Structure

The app will emulate the career of a fighter. Users will be able to train, accept fights, sign contracts, and manage expenses.
Once the features were defined I started putting together the wireframes and interactions.

Visual

I started sketching out ideas for the logo. I wanted it to replicate a cage for cage fighting. I am unable to use an octagon since it's trademarked by the UFC. I also created a pinterest folder for my mood board. The stakeholder wants to use a purple hue for the branding.
