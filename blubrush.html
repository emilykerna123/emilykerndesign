Blu Brush

Introduction

Blu Brush is a sonic toothbrush that tracks movement in real time as you brush your teeth. By using Blu Brush with it's app users can make sure every spot in their mouth is brushed. The client wanted to redesign their brand and this includes updating the app to match their new vision.

Challenge

The main challenge is to update the app design to match their new brand "Blu". The Client is redesigning their toothbrush to be sleeker and more sophisticated, so it was important to design an app that portrays this. In addition to a new design, the client requested a new color palette. I wanted to make sure this palette would be inline with the new brand direction.

Research

I did some research on the market and there are quite a few competitors. They are all pretty similar so I did a competitive analysis to see what is currently on the market. In addition, I reviewed layouts and designs of other apps in the market to see what competitors are doing. Since we were updating the colors I researched color theory to see what the best choice would be. The three qualities I feel fit the brand the most are loyalty, trust, and refreshing.
