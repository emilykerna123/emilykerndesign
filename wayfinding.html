I was tasked with redesigning our company wayfinding solution and wanted to create something that was simple, modern, and easy to navigate for users on the go.

Challenge

The challenge was the create something that the developers could translate into a product. This meant that each item has to be able to be customized and work for various potential clients. The fonts, colors, and images had to be flexible and work regardless of the branding. I included icons and illustrations for images in case the client doesn't have assets to provide. This allows for clients to select from illustration options and not feel the need to have images for everything.
