Theming Process

Overview

I created the theming process for the Product Design System at Phunware. The approach I defined included documenting and implementing theming into our existing design system and allowing for customization at a configuration level. This process has enabled the team to quickly roll out new components for the design system and reduced the design process time for applying custom branding by 50%.
